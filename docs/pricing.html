FAQ

Common questions.

How do I get a Pro key?

Join the Discord, open a ticket in #how-to-get-pro or DM Grant directly. Tell us your platform (macOS or Windows). Most keys are issued within 24 hours.

Does it cost money?

No payment processor, no subscription. Keys are issued by hand. If you want to support the project, the best thing you can do is post about SpoofTrap on TikTok, YouTube, or Reddit and share the link in #community-content — that earns you a Pro key on the spot.

How many devices does one key activate?

Two. You can use one Mac and one Windows machine, or two of either. If you need to move to a new computer, ask in Discord and we'll deactivate the old one.

What if my key stops working?

Open a ticket in Discord and we'll fix it.
